History

The IT Department was established in 2010 with the goal of providing students with a strong foundation in technology and sigital innovation. In the early years, the department offered basic computer science and programming courses to build students' technical literacy. As technology rapidly evolved, the department expanded its curriculum to include areas such as web development, networking, cybersecurity, and data analytics.

Over the years, our department has grown into a dynamic community of learners and educators. Through collaboration with industries and continuous curriculum enhancement, we have produced skilled graduates of the digital world. Today, the IT Department continues to evolve, embracing innovation and preparing students for the future of technology.
