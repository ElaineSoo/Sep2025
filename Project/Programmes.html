Diploma in Computer Science

This programme introduces students to the fundamental concepts of computing, including programming, data structures, algorithms, and database systems. Student will also gain practical experience in software development and system analysis. Graduates will be well-prepared for careers in software engineering, IT support, or furthur studies at the bachelor's level.
