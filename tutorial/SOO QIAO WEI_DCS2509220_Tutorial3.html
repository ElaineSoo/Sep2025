Short Story: The Lion and the Mouse


One day, a lion was asleep in the wood. A mouse came along. The mouse didn't see the lion and ran across the lion's nose.

The lion woke up. He was cross with the mouse."You woke me up!" he roared.

The mouse was scared. "Plese let me go!" said the mouse. "If you let me go, I will repay you one day!"

"You are funny!"said the lion. "How could a mouse help a lion?"

The lion laughed and let the house go home.

Some days later, the lion was caught in a hunter's net. He couldn't get out.

The lion roared in anger.

The mouse heard the lion and ran to the net.

The mouse saw the lion and had an idea. She chewed on the net until it broke and the lion fell out. The lion was free.

"Even a mouse can help a lion!" said the mouse.

From the day, the mouse and the lion became friends.


Moral:A kindness is never wasted.
